Where Are Calls Routed?

Freedom From Addiction is not a Treatment Center. All calls are randomly routed to an available Treatment Center Provider.

Freedom From Addiction makes the best effort to ensure that a Treatment Center provider is available to answer your call twenty four hours a day.
‍
All calls are 100% FREE, private and confidential.

About Freedom From Addiction

Freedom From Addiction facilitates the means of bridging individuals and families suffering from drug addiction and alcohol abuse with substance abuse treatment centers.

FFA provides direct connection to treatment center representatives whom can guide each individual on the initial step towards a lifetime of recovery.
